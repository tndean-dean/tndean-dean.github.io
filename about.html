About This Guide

This website was created to help new players learn and enjoy Monster Hunter Wilds. When first starting the game, many players can feel overwhelmed by the amount of mechanics, weapons, and systems available. This guide aimed to simplify that experience by providing clear, organized, and beginner-friendly information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are some weapons stronger than others?

A: Yes, different weapons have unique strengths and weaknesses depending on the monster you're facing, but all are viable.

Q: What weapon should I start with?

A: Sword & Shield or Long Sword are great beginner options.

Q: I can't seem to beat this monster, what should I do?

A: Try different weapons or strategies. Also, ensure you are upgrading your gear according to your Hunter Rank and plan for the monster's weaknesses/strengths.

Q: I always seem to run out of materials, what should I do?

A: Make sure to gather materials in the field and complete quests that reward useful items. Also, consider farming specific monsters for the materials you need. As you progress, you will also unlock the ability to have the villagers gather materials for you, which can be a huge help.
